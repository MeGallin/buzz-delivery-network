Who the BuzZ are we?

Online business has become the most cost effective form of trade. Online ordering eliminates large, fixed, overhead and operating costs. It opens bottlenecks and enables new revenue pipelines, while providing efficiency in your business.

Having your product displayed online means that anyone can purchase/request it without being limited to your specific location.

The BuzZ Delivery Network enables on-demand delivery business and online e-commerce business. We enable existing business and new entrepreneurs to start their own online delivery and/or e-commerce pipelines.
