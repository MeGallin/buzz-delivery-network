Delivery Agents

Buzz Network - (Agents)- Where agents have developed their own network of providers and a supporting market, vendors may participate and derive benefit from the turnovers that are generated by the agent’s customers by subscribing to their local business and advertising on their website and marketing collateral.

Vendors will be exposed to the database of customers and have opportunity to market to them.

In today modern world we are entering an era where physical stores are fading out due to the cost of physical infrastructure plus the growing trend for online shopping. The link between online stores and consumers is the delivery service. The Buzz Delivery Network is creating a nationwide footprint of businesses that do deliveries. These businesses will be made available to you in order for them to deliver your products. If there is not a network delivery service in your area then an outsourced service will be made available to you.

You however always have the choice to choose your own delivery service as the network is there to support you and does not prescribe which vendors you make use of.
